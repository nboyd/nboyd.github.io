Nick Boyd

I do machine learning at a DNA sequencing startup.

I did my undergrad at UC Berkeley majoring in math and computer science and working with Dan Klein. I stayed for my PhD in statistics supervised by Michael Jordan and Ben Recht. Before joining XGenomes I was briefly a postdoc with Aviv Regev at the Broad Institute.

I'm interested in algorithms for optimization, and applications of optimization and machine learning to inverse problems in biology, computational imaging, and physics.
